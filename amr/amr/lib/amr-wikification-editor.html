AMR Wikification Editor guidelines       Ulf Hermjakob     Oct. 10, 2014

Login to AMR Editor

  • Go to http://www.isi.edu/amr/amr/AMR-editor.html
  • Login in with your personal AMR Editor username and the password that you selected
  • Bookmark the AMR Editor as http://www.isi.edu/amr/amr/AMR-editor.html (not as ...cgi)

Load workset and start wikification editor

  • On AMR Editor main page, click on blue load button.
  • Fill in annotator (often group rather than personal): cjconsensus | colorado | ldcpreferred | rpi
  • Fill in workset name. If you don't remember the exact workset name, leave blank, and later select from menu.
  • Click on gray Load workset at ISI button.
  • After you are in the right workset, click on blue wiki button. This will dynamically generate an AMR Wikification Editor page. That might take several seconds.

Inside the AMR Wikification Editor

  • At the top you will find a status summary and brief instructions.
  • Named entities to be wikified are shown on the right.
  • Underlined items (full or dotted) are sensitive to mouse-over and click operations.
  • Click on an underlined named entity (NE) to invoke a mini-menu for that NE. If the underline is dashed, no RPI wikification is available.
    • Select the correct wikification if available.
    • You can mouse-over RPI suggestions to see a brief summary of the wikipedia entry to help make sure it's the right one.
    • Click on the Google icon ("G") to search Google in wikipedia for the NE. Cut and paste the wiki title from any successful Google search into the AMR Wikification Editor, which will automatically do minor reformatting (e.g. replace spaces by underscores).
    • Select/type in a simple dash ("-") if no wiki page exists for the named entity.
    • The wiki page you select should not be a redirection page or a disambiguation page.
  • If you are not sure how to wikify, or if you think that the underlying named entity is incorrect (wrong type, wrong scope, or shouldn't even be a named entity in the first place), use a question mark ("?") as a wikification value. This signals a problem case to a wikification expert. If there is no wiki page, choose a simple dash ("-").
  • Super important: Save your wikifications (at bottom of page). You don't have to wikify everything on the page before saving.
